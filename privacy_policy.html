Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026  ·  Operator: 7th Dimension Media  ·  Contact: info@7thdimensionmarkets.com

This Privacy Policy describes how 7th Dimension Media ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use Earth Scanner and related services at 7thdimensionmarkets.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information depending on how you use our services:

  • Email address — when you subscribe to shock alerts, sign up for a Pro subscription, or authenticate via Google OAuth.
  • Payment information — billing is processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number, CVV, or full payment details on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Usage data — we may log the date and time of Terms of Service acceptance, along with IP address and browser/device type, for legal compliance and abuse prevention.
  • Google account information — if you sign in with Google (Pro users), we receive your name and email address from Google solely for authentication and subscription management.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you shock alert emails you have subscribed to
  • To verify and manage your subscription status
  • To authenticate your access to Earth Scanner Pro
  • To comply with legal obligations (Terms of Service records)
  • To detect and prevent abuse or unauthorized access

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription management
  • Supabase — secure database hosting for subscriber records
  • SendGrid (Twilio) — email delivery for shock alerts
  • Google — OAuth authentication for Pro users
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users

All third-party services are bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

4. Email Communications

  • Alert subscribers receive shock alert emails based on market conditions
  • You may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us at info@7thdimensionmarkets.com
  • We will never send unsolicited promotional emails to non-subscribers
  • Transactional emails (subscription confirmations, payment receipts) may be sent regardless of marketing preferences

5. Data Retention

  • Subscriber records are retained for the duration of your subscription and up to 2 years after cancellation for legal and accounting purposes
  • Terms of Service acceptance logs are retained for 5 years
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us

6. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure database hosting via Supabase, and restricted API access. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable precautions to protect your information.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@7thdimensionmarkets.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Earth Scanner is built on Streamlit and hosted on Streamlit Cloud. The platform may use session cookies for authentication and state management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Earth Scanner is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
